CYCLING 2024:

YOUR PATH TO VICTORY

The cycling race is an exciting competition for cycling enthusiasts, where each participant can test themselves on tracks of varying difficulty.

about the race

  • history and mission
    Cycling 2024 was created to unite cycling enthusiasts of all levels and provide them with a unique opportunity to test their strength on various tracks. Our mission is to popularize a healthy lifestyle and develop cycling in the region.
  • trails and routes
    Our routes wind through scenic forests, hills and fields, offering participants unforgettable views and challenges. Choose the distance that suits you - from 10km for beginners to 100km for experienced riders.

To the participants

  • HOW TO TAKE PART
    To participate in the race you need to register on our website, pay the fee and confirm your participation. After that you will receive all necessary instructions and a participant number.
  • Rules and requirements
    All participants must wear helmets and obey the rules of the road. A detailed list of rules is available in the 'Rules' section
  • Equipment and training
    We recommend using good quality bikes and sportswear. Check out our preparation tips to help you feel confident on the course.
